Adult BMI & BMR Tracker
=============================

A simple and interactive  Tracker built with Streamlit to help calculate BMI (Body Mass Index) and BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate).

Note  : This Tracker for adults only(19+years)

BMI
Calculates Body Mass Index to check weight status and health category. Provides short advice based on the BMI result.

** Formula**
BMI = weight (kg) / (height (m))²

BMR
BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the calories your body needs at rest to keep you alive.

Formula (Mifflin–St Jeor Equation):
For Men : BMR = (10weight)+(6.25height)-(5*age)+5

For Women: BMR = (10weight)+(6.25height)-(5*age)-161
